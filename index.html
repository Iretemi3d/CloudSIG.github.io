What is Cloud?

There is no fluffy cloud in the Cloud, it’s just someone else’s computer. You can manage it remotely and use a variety of tools to optimize time and cost. We’ll show you how the cloud is essential for companies like Netflix and AI platforms, and run short workshops where you can practice cloud networking yourself.
